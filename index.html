About the Event

Weekend of Code, the annual technical event of MNNIT, Allahabad is up with its 2k21 edition! The enthralling week long event consists of Devjam and Codestart. Devjam aims at providing a glimpse of the world of development to our freshmen while Codestart is a coding event for our freshmen and sophomores. The sole purpose of this event is to provide participants with hands-on experience and exposure along with proper guidance. Get ready for an adventurous journey of learning, innovating and exploring new technologies.

  • Week dedicated to coding and development
  • Meet Developers and Coders from your batch
  • Get Inspired by Amazing Mentors
  • Make project in any language you want
  • Test your coding skills with 5 hour insomaniac event "CodeStart"
  • Completely Remote Edition
  • Location Your home
  • Event duration May 22nd,2021 - May 29th,2021
  • Mentors10+ Mentors (still counting)
  • DevelopersThe more the merrier :)

Why should you join?

Participating in WoC will go a long way towards developing your technical skills. This event will be a great opportunity for freshmen to get started with developing and coding. You don’t need any prior knowledge to participate in this event (except zeal for learning!!) because your mentor will guide you through every step. At the end of this event you will have a great project. Coding and developing skills will also help you in getting an internship and placement. Don’t miss out this opportunity and discover the fun in coding and developing.
